03 — How We Work

Four steps. No surprises.

Step 01

Conversation

We start with a 20-minute call or email exchange. What's working, what isn't, what you actually want from a website. No pitch, no slides — just a real conversation about your business.

Step 02

Written proposal

Within 48 hours, you get a written proposal: scope, timeline, fixed price, payment terms. One number, no surprises. 50% deposit, 50% on delivery.

Step 03

Build & preview

Two to four weeks of focused build work. You get a live preview link as soon as the first draft is ready. Two rounds of revisions included. We iterate until it's right.

Step 04

Launch & support

Site goes live, final invoice clears, and you're done. Optional monthly maintenance from $100 covers hosting, security, and small updates. No long-term contracts.
