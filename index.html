— how it goes —

Four steps. No surprises.

Step one

A real conversation

We start with a 20-minute call or email exchange. What's working, what isn't, what you actually want from a website. No pitch, no slides — just an honest conversation about your business.

Step two

A written proposal

Within 48 hours, you get a written proposal: scope, timeline, fixed price, payment terms. One number, no surprises. Half deposit, half on delivery.

Step three

Build & preview

Two to four weeks of focused build work. You get a live preview link as soon as the first draft is ready. Two rounds of revisions included. We iterate until it's right.

Step four

Launch & support

The site goes live, the final invoice clears, and you're done. Optional monthly maintenance from $100 keeps things sharp. No long-term contracts.
