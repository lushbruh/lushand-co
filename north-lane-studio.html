— our approach

Why small classes, always.

"Twelve mats is enough. The point is for the teacher to actually see you, your knees, your shoulders, the moment you stopped breathing."

North Lane was opened in 2019 with one practical rule: no class would ever exceed twelve mats. Most studios run 20-30, because the math works better. We made the math work differently — slightly higher prices, fewer classes per day, teachers paid for their attention rather than their reach.

The result is a studio that feels small. People know each other. Teachers know hands and hips and what your back was doing last week. New people come back. That's what we built it for.
